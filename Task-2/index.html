About Us

Founded in 2020, Brewed Awakening was born from a passion for exceptional coffee and a desire to create a welcoming space for our community. Our master baristas carefully select and roast the finest beans from sustainable sources worldwide, ensuring each cup meets our exacting standards.

We believe that great coffee has the power to bring people together and make every day a little bit better. Our cozy atmosphere and friendly staff create the perfect environment for catching up with friends, getting some work done, or simply enjoying a moment of solitude with your favorite brew.
